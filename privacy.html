Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2025

Welcome to TeesHub. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you visit our portfolio demo store. Since this website is meant for showcasing technical skills, we do not collect or sell any personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration or sign-in is required.
  • No payment details are collected.
  • No personal identifiers like name, phone number, or address are requested.

Cookies and LocalStorage

We use browser LocalStorage to simulate cart functionality and theme preferences. These are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

This site does not integrate with third-party analytics, payment processors, or social media trackers.

Your Rights

Since no data is collected, there’s no data to correct or delete. If you clear your browser storage, any saved preferences or cart items will be removed.
